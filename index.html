About

Who we are

NOVA GOAL COMPANY LIMITED, established on January 21, 2024, specializes primarily in personnel dispatch, web development, and study abroad support. We harness innovative approaches and technical expertise to provide high-quality solutions tailored to our clients' needs. Particularly, we contribute to the growth and development of businesses through personnel dispatch and project outsourcing between Japan and Myanmar.

Our Mission

NOVA GOAL COMPANY LIMITED is dedicated to serving as a bridge for talent and technology between Japan and Myanmar, fostering economic development and cultural exchange between the two nations. We prioritize trust and quality, striving to consistently exceed customer expectations by providing exceptional services.

Services

What we do offer

Introduction of Myanmar Personnel Dispatch

Through our partnership with Myanmar Narita, we provide a service to introduce Myanmar talent who can work in Japan. We connect Myanmar professionals with excellent technical skills and language proficiency to companies in Japan, fulfilling the manpower needs of both countries.

Study Abroad Support

We offer support for individuals and students who wish to study in Japan. Through various assistance services such as preparation for enrollment, procedures, and lifestyle support, we facilitate a smooth experience for international students.

Web Development Project Outsourcing

We undertake web development projects commissioned by Japanese web development companies, utilizing ASP.NET and ASP.NET Core technologies. Our proficient development team in Myanmar delivers high-quality solutions, contributing to the success of the projects.
